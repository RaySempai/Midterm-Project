Welcome to D'Rey Pasta!

About Us

Our restaurant, D'Rey Pasta, serves classic and fresh meals that combine the best of traditional Italian recipes with fresh, local ingredients.
Opened in 1994, D'Rey Pasta is celebrated as one of downtown Brooklyn oldest family-owned and operated restaurants running in its original location. Handing down cherished Italian traditions as it's main ingredients, D'Rey Pasta menu pairs generations of authenticity and love with freshness and flavor.
